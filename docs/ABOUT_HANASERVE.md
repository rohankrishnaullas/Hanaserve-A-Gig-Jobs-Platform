## How It Works

### Provider Flow
1. Start a conversation with Hana (chat or voice)
2. Describe your skills, availability, and service area naturally
3. Hana creates your flexible provider profile automatically
4. Receive matched service requests when requesters need your skills

### Requester Flow
1. Describe what you need to Hana in natural language
2. Hana understands your request, including implicit requirements
3. Get matched with capable local providers
4. Choose to let Hana handle coordination or select providers yourself
